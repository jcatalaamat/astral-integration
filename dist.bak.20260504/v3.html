· A note from the studio ·

The practice we run.

You inspire hundreds of people in a weekend, and then spend the next three days fighting five disconnected tools. Calendly for bookings. Teachable for courses. Stripe for payments. Mailchimp for the list. WhatsApp for the community. Six platforms paying themselves rent in your name, and not one of them feels like the work.

The Linktree is not the problem — it is the symptom. The problem is that nobody built you a home. Six different companies are holding your relationships hostage, each optimizing for its own metrics, not yours. The most valuable thing you have is the relationship with your people, and it has been quietly fractured into fragments scattered across software that was never designed to talk to each other.

Our role is not to invent the work. It is to support what is already real with structure that fits. We read what already exists. We identify what matters now. We help the work take a form that others can understand and engage with — without losing what made it worth building in the first place.

We build systems, not one-off deliverables. We work with founders who already have something real in motion — communities, practitioners, programs, schools, retreat centers, organizations — and we replace the rented stack with one custom platform that does exactly what their work needs. Aligned partnership from day one. No upfront. We share what you make. Your wins are our wins.

— Jordi Amat Mazunte · April 2026

Clear, grounded sites. Editorial pages. Brand systems. Bilingual when needed. Custom code, no templates — built around the work, not stuffed into a theme.

Layer 02 · Offer structures

Service menus, tiered pricing, intake routing, application gates, custom-order pathways. Discovery to engagement, without friction — and without losing the person to a cart.

Course delivery, member portals, practitioner directories, curriculum engines, AI assistants trained on your methodology. Scalable systems built with intention.

Layer 04 · Operational systems

Booking calendars, Stripe Connect, email automation, internal dashboards, API integrations. The pipes that move the work — without the relationship getting lost in them.
