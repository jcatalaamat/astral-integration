For Makers & Artisans

Your craft deserves a home that honors the work.

You make beautiful things — bespoke robes, plant-dyed garments, ceramics, prints, long-form writing. Your work doesn't belong on an Etsy template that takes 6.5% and treats your craft like inventory. It needs a digital home that breathes the texture of what you make.

The Problem

Marketplaces flatten the craft.

Etsy

6.5% transaction fee + listing fees + ad costs. Your work next to mass-produced lookalikes. Their algorithm decides who sees you.

Shopify default

Built for high-velocity e-commerce. Your bespoke piece treated like a SKU. Theme that looks like everyone else's.

Instagram only

No checkout, no inventory, no email list, no archive. The platform owns the relationship — you rent the audience.

Slow craft needs slow infrastructure. A site that doesn't rush. A pace that matches the work. Buying that begins with finding the right piece, not pushing through a funnel.

What We Build

A site that reads like a gallery.

Not a shop. A digital home for the craft — process, materials, pieces, story. Buying happens; it just isn't the centerpiece.

01

A site that feels like the work

Not Etsy. Not Shopify's default. A custom-designed home that breathes the texture of what you make — generous space, slow typography, intentional rhythm. The site is the first impression of the craft.

02

Your catalog, presented as art

Each piece gets its own page, its own story, its own pace. Photography, materials, process, price. Not a product grid optimized for conversion — a gallery designed for the right person to find the right piece.

03

Stripe checkout · no marketplace fees

Direct sales. You set the price, you keep the brand, you own the relationship. Stripe takes their 2.9%. Etsy keeps 6.5% + listing fees + ad costs. We replace that.

04

Inventory + custom orders

Track what's available, what's reserved, what's sold. For bespoke work, a custom-order pathway that begins with a conversation, not a cart.

05

Newsletter + gentle re-engagement

When a new collection drops, your people hear about it first. Email capture built in, list grows organically. No Mailchimp template — your voice, your rhythm.

06

You own everything

Your code lives in your GitHub. Your domain is in your name. Your customer list is in your database. If we ever stop working together, you walk away with the keys. No platform holding your craft hostage.
