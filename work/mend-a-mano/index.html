Mend a Mano

Textile Artisan — Bespoke Robes & Goods

View Live

The Challenge

A textile artisan crafting bespoke robes from linen and wool — no digital presence beyond word of mouth. Each garment takes weeks to make, and the work needed a digital home as intentional as the cloth.

Key Decisions

Off-the-shelf e-commerce templates assume inventory and turnover. This work is the opposite: slow, bespoke, conversation-driven. We chose an editorial approach — process and material first, products second, custom orders through direct contact rather than checkout. Linen texture overlay, earthy palette, generous space.

What Was Built

A site that feels like the cloth: linen-textured, paced, intentional. Product showcase, process storytelling, and a custom-order pathway that begins with a conversation, not a cart.
