# audit: /about (AboutPage)

## verdict: strong personal page. some bloat in practitioner section.

### what works
- photo + bio side-by-side is clean
- "family is the reason I build the way I do" section humanizes without oversharing
- journey narrative (london > barcelona > cdmx > mazunte) is compelling
- practitioner credentials list is differentiated and honest
- technical background cards are solid
- stats section (10+ years, 8 platforms, 2-6 wk, mazunte) is good social proof

### issues

1. **practitioner section is too long**
   - the paragraph at line 172 lists every modality (access bars, theta healing, reiki, gene keys, family constellations, ashtanga, vinyasa, yin, kundalini...). this reads like a resume, not a story.
   - **rewrite**: "I've trained across modalities — from Access Bars to breathwork to ceremony. Not because I planned to build platforms for this world, but because the work called. Each training changed how I understand what practitioners actually need from their infrastructure."
   - remove the full enumeration. let the "why it matters" card carry the specifics.

2. **"artists, creatives, changemakers, practitioners, facilitators, and organizations" appears 3 times**
   - line 172, line 349, and the meta description. this 6-word list is becoming a verbal tic across the entire site. replace with shorter phrases contextually:
   - "the people doing the most original work" (already used once)
   - "the work itself" 
   - or just "practitioners, teachers, and organizations" depending on context

3. **CTA section is generic**
   - "Let's talk about your work." — used on multiple pages
   - **rewrite**: "I take on a small number of partnerships at a time. If your work needs infrastructure that doesn't exist yet, let's talk."
   - (this is already close to what's there but tighter)

4. **stats: "8 Platforms built"** — is this current? cross-check with actual case studies count. if there are 8 case studies in the data file, it matches. but it should stay in sync.

5. **og url: astralintegration.co** — rebrand

### copy rewrites

**practitioner modality paragraph** (replace line 172):
```
I've trained across modalities — from Access Bars to breathwork to 
ceremony work. Not because I planned to build platforms for this world, 
but because the work called. Each training changed how I understand 
what practitioners actually need from their infrastructure.
```

### structural fixes
- trim practitioner section by ~40%
- consolidate the repeated 6-word audience list
