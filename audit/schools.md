# audit: /schools (SchoolsPage)

## verdict: strong. the progression visualization is the standout element.

### what works
- student progression UI mockup is genuinely impressive — shows don't tell
- "this isn't a mockup. this is what I build." line lands hard
- teachable comparison is specific and credible (lists 6 things teachable can't do)
- inner ascend case study fits perfectly (97 practices, facilitator pipeline)
- system capabilities list is concrete, not vague

### issues

1. **pricing inconsistency** — meta description says "12% rev share, Membership tier" but the pricing card shows "5-15%". should show "12%" to match the tier.
   - **fix**: pricing card value = "12%", detail = "Membership tier · from dollar one"

2. **"The Honest Question" section title again** — same label as every other audience page
   - **fix**: "The Question Every School Asks"

3. **"schools that are growing" in pricing section h2** — slightly presumptuous. what about schools that are just starting?
   - **rewrite**: "Built around how schools actually work."

4. **certification visualization could be interactive** — currently static. adding a click/hover to show "what unlocks at each level" would increase engagement. but this is a feature request, not an audit fix.

5. **no live site link for inner ascend** — the case study card doesn't have a "view live" link. is inner ascend a private portal?

6. **og url: astralintegration.co** — rebrand

### copy fixes
- pricing card: "5-15%" → "12%", detail: "Membership tier · from dollar one"
- h2 "Built for schools that are growing" → "Built around how schools actually work."
