# audit: /practitioners (PractitionersPage)

## verdict: strongest audience page after makers. diagnostic checklist is a smart CRO move.

### what works
- interactive diagnostic checklist is excellent — gets visitors to self-identify and self-qualify
- "2 out of 6" dynamic feedback is smart micro-copy
- before/after grid is clean and immediately scannable
- "why not just hire a developer?" objection handling is honest and specific
- inner ascend case study is well placed as proof
- form CTA "Send me your site. I'll tell you what's broken." is the strongest CTA on the entire site

### issues

1. **hero uses "I" correctly** — good. but subhead mixes: "I replace all of it" and later "No pitch. No pressure." — consistent.

2. **pricing section shows "5-15%"** but the meta description says "8% rev share". which is it? the pricing page says Service tier = 8%. the page body should match.
   - **fix**: change the pricing card to show "8%" as the headline value, with "Service tier" as detail. currently showing the generic 5-15% range which undermines the specificity of having named tiers.

3. **"The Honest Question" section title** — used identically on schools, retreats, communities, organizations. every audience page has a section called "The Honest Question." the copy underneath is different and good, but the section label is formulaic.
   - **fix**: vary the section labels. for practitioners: "The Question You're Already Asking"

4. **no calendly link on the form** — the makers page has "or Book a call directly" next to submit. practitioners page has it in the form but missing the calendly link styling. check: actually it's not there at all. the form just has a submit button.
   - **fix**: add "or book a call directly" link next to submit button, matching makers pattern

5. **diagnostic results not sent in form** — wait, they ARE sent (lines 78-96). good. the checked items get included in the email. smart.

6. **og url: astralintegration.co** — rebrand

### copy fixes

**pricing card**: change value from "5-15%" to "8%" with detail "Service tier · from dollar one"

**section label**: "The Honest Question" → "The Question You're Already Asking"
