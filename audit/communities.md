# audit: /communities (CommunitiesPage)

## verdict: solid. good differentiation vs facebook groups.

### what works
- "every town needs a digital backbone" — strong opening
- problem section (whatsapp groups, instagram stories, word of mouth) names the real pain
- platform layers (events, directory, classifieds, digest) are concrete
- mazunte today case study is perfect proof — real URL, bilingual, daily updates
- facebook groups objection handling is well argued
- bilingual callout box is a differentiator

### issues

1. **pricing inconsistency** — meta says "12% rev share, Membership tier" but card shows "5-15%"
   - **fix**: show "12%" with "Membership tier"

2. **"The Honest Question" section** — same title pattern. 
   - **fix**: "The Question Every Organizer Asks"

3. **revenue model is unclear for community platforms** — communities typically don't have direct revenue the way a practitioner or school does. how does the rev share work? from directory listings? premium placements? ad revenue? the pricing card says "5-15% from dollar one" but doesn't explain what revenue is being shared. this needs a clarifying line.
   - **add**: "Revenue share applies to paid listings, premium directory placements, and sponsored events. Free community features stay free."

4. **no digest example** — the platform layers describe a "weekly digest" but there's no example of what it looks like. a screenshot or mockup would strengthen this.

5. **form label says "Community / Location"** — good contextual label. this is well done.

6. **og url: astralintegration.co** — rebrand

### copy fixes
- pricing section: add clarifying line about what revenue is shared for community platforms
- pricing card: "5-15%" → "12%"
