The Full Journey

From inquiry to integration.

The platform handles every phase — not just the booking.

01

Discovery

Guest finds your center. Each offering has its own page, its own story, its own booking flow — not a generic grid.

02

Intake

Custom intake per offering type. A retreat asks about medical history and intentions. A homestay asks about dates and dietary needs. The questions match the depth of the work.

03

Preparation

After booking, automated preparation sequences — packing lists, dietary guidelines, intention prompts, logistical details. Timed and customized by offering.

04

Experience

Capacity management, check-in, on-site coordination. You run the retreat. The platform handles the logistics.

05

Integration

Post-retreat resources, integration guidance, follow-up sequences. Membership invitations for ongoing connection. The relationship continues.

The Honest Question

"Why not use Airbnb or Booking.com?"

Because your work isn't hospitality. Here's what those platforms assume:

They assume

  • ×Anyone with a credit card can book
  • ×All rooms are interchangeable
  • ×The stay is the product
  • ×No preparation needed
  • ×Checkout = goodbye

Your reality

  • ✓Guests need screening and preparation
  • ✓Each offering has different requirements
  • ✓The experience needs context before arrival
  • ✓Prep materials are part of the container
  • ✓Integration continues after they leave

Plus: every booking on Airbnb costs you 15-20% in commissions. Direct bookings through your platform cost you zero in platform fees.
