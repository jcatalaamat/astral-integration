Pricing

Clear pricing. No surprises.

Every partnership is scoped to the work. Here's how pricing works so you know what to expect before we talk.

Every engagement has two parts:

1. Foundation Build

The initial platform that replaces your scattered tools with one cohesive system. This is a one-time investment based on scope.

2. Monthly Partnership

Ongoing technical leadership, maintenance, new features, and platform evolution. This is what makes it a partnership, not a project.
