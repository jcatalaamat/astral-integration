✦ The Builder

the builder,
briefly.

senior full-stack engineer. former CTO. i spent a decade building production systems and leading engineering teams in startups you've probably heard of. then i moved to Mazunte, Oaxaca — a small village on the pacific coast of mexico — and started building for the people doing what i consider the most original and under-served work in the world: education, healing, ceremony, and community.

i live inside the same world my clients do. breathwork circles, cacao ceremonies, indigenous oaxacan traditions, plant medicine, sound, silence, early mornings with coffee and a journal, late nights with a terminal. i don't build for this world from the outside. i build from inside it.

the technical competence is old — a decade of shipping production systems — but the speed is new. i work with AI tools that let me move at a velocity that used to require a full team. and i charge a fraction of what an agency would because my overhead is a hammock, a satellite dish, and a cup of Oaxacan coffee.

i don't do sales calls. i don't send proposals. i find people whose work i respect, i build them something real, and i send them the link. if they like it, we work together. if not, they keep the build anyway.

— the principle

they were never separate processes. building and healing are the same service — listening on different ports.
